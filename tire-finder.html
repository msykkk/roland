Can't Find Your Vehicle?

If your vehicle isn't listed in our tool, here are other ways to find your tire size:

  • Check your current tires - The size is printed on the sidewall
  • Vehicle door jamb - Look for a sticker on the driver's side door
  • Owner's manual - Tire specifications are usually listed
  • Call us - We can look it up for you over the phone

Understanding Tire Sizes

Tire sizes might look confusing, but they're actually quite logical:

Example: 225/60R16
  • 225 = Width in millimeters
  • 60 = Aspect ratio (sidewall height)
  • R = Radial construction
  • 16 = Wheel diameter in inches
