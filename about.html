Our Story

Roland's Tire and Automotive has been a cornerstone of the Millington community since 1965. What started as a small tire shop has grown into a full-service automotive repair facility, but our commitment to honest, reliable service has never changed.

As a family-owned and operated business, we understand the importance of trust when it comes to your vehicle. That's why we've built our reputation on transparency, fair pricing, and quality workmanship. We treat every customer like family and every vehicle as if it were our own.

Over the years, we've invested in the latest diagnostic equipment and continuous training for our technicians, ensuring we can service all makes and models with expertise and efficiency.

Our Mission
