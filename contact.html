Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?

While we accept walk-ins, we recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure the fastest service. Call us or use our contact form to schedule.

Do you work on all vehicle makes?

Yes! Our ASE-certified technicians service all makes and models, both foreign and domestic vehicles.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, check, and all major credit cards. We also offer financing options for larger repairs.

Do you offer warranties?

Yes, we provide warranties on both parts and labor. Warranty terms vary by service - ask for details.

Can I wait while my car is serviced?

Absolutely! We have a comfortable waiting area with complimentary coffee and Wi-Fi.

Do you provide estimates?

Yes, we provide free estimates and will never perform work without your approval.
